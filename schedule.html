Referral Program

The strength of HiiTFit is that before exercises, before results.....we are a fitness family first. It is this family that we are thrilled that you have become a part of and would love your help to keep our family growing. Simply, for EVERY person you refer to HiiTFit who joins our family, you will receive $10 off of your next month's dues. There is NO LIMIT to how much can be taken off your next month's dues. Refer enough people, guess what.....you get an ENTIRELY FREE MONTH!!!

Discount Program

Here at HiiTFit we give discounts to some key groups in our community. The following groups receive a 20% discount at all times off of any HiiTFit package; Military, Law Enforcement, First Responders, SDOC (School District of Osceola County), and Family Member Discounts. Does not stack with other discounts.

HiiTFit Class

HiiTFit classes are group interval training classes that are challenging and energetic, where we strive to improve each individual's strength, speed, performance, and overall athleticism.

Extended HiiTFit Class

The Saturday morning Extended HiiTFit class is 15 minutes longer than our standard 1-hour class where we are able to program some additional training elements into this awesome weekend class.
